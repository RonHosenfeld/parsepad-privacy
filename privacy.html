ParsePad Privacy Policy

A format-aware text editor for macOS

Data Collection

ParsePad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All files and content you create or edit remain entirely on your device.

Network Usage

ParsePad does not connect to the internet. The app operates completely offline. There are no analytics, tracking, telemetry, or external service calls of any kind.

Third-Party Services

ParsePad does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data.

Your Files

Any documents you open, create, or edit in ParsePad are stored locally on your Mac in locations you choose. ParsePad does not copy, upload, or share your files.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be posted here and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
